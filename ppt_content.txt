**PARAMETER CHOICES & JUSTIFICATION**:

**1. Adam Optimizer**
- Alasan: Adaptive learning rate per parameter
- Alternatif: SGD (terlalu slow), RMSprop (kurang stable)
- Learning rate: 0.001 (default yang proven)

**2. Categorical Crossentropy Loss**
- Alasan: Standard untuk multi-class classification
- Formula: L = -Î£(y_true Ã— log(y_pred))
- Cocok untuk softmax output

**3. Class Weighting**
- Problem: Imbalanced dataset (Happiness 1774 vs Fear 281)
- Solution: Inverse frequency weighting
- Formula: weight = n_samples / (n_classes Ã— n_samples_per_class)
- Impact: Improved recall pada minority classes

**4. Dropout Rate**
- Conv layers: 0.25 (preserve spatial features)
- Dense layer: 0.5 (strong regularization)
- Prevent overfitting pada small dataset
